One Piece

A story of a young priate named monkey d. luffy and his crew, the Straw hat pirates who are on the search for the legendray, "One Piece". Luffy is striving to become king of the pirates he has a long journey to go with a ton of challenges to face and overcome. along with that one piece conveys the importance of freedom and fighting for it within all the characters involved. While promoting freedom, the series also highlights the dangers of oppressive forces like the World Government.
